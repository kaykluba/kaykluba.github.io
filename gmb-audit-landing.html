Questions

Everything you need to know

Is this audit really free?
Yes — completely free, no credit card required, no account needed. You enter your GBP URL, get your score on screen instantly, and can download the full PDF report by entering your email. We offer it free because a significant number of businesses that see their score choose to work with us to fix their profile.
How accurate is the AI audit?
The audit is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic) and analyses your profile based on Google's documented ranking factors and industry benchmarks. It provides directional scoring and the most impactful optimisation opportunities. For a pixel-perfect technical audit with live data pulls from the Google API, our paid audit service provides full precision.
What do I do with the score?
Start with fix number one in your priority list and work down. Most Quick Win fixes can be implemented in under 30 minutes directly in your Google Business Profile dashboard. High Impact fixes (like building a review acquisition system) require a more systematic approach — which is where our managed service delivers the biggest results.
Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?
Yes — we serve businesses in Nigeria, the UK, and the US. The audit tool works for any Google Business Profile globally. Our managed service operates across Lagos, Abuja, London, Manchester, New York, Chicago, Miami, and all major markets in our three core regions.
What happens after I enter my email for the PDF?
Your PDF downloads immediately. You'll also receive a follow-up email with your top fix broken down in more detail, and a brief email sequence with GMB optimisation tips over the following week. You can unsubscribe at any time. We never share your data with third parties.
How long does GMB optimisation take to show results?
Quick wins like category updates, description rewrites, and photo uploads can show Map Pack movement within 2–4 weeks. Review velocity improvements typically take 30–60 days to reflect in rankings. Citation corrections take 60–90 days to propagate. Our Nigerian market clients see results fastest due to lower competitive baselines.
